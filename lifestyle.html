Tourist Attractions

There are lot of beautiful places to visit in Cape Town such as the beaches, Robben Island and Table mountain. These places also involve a lot activities for families to enjoy such as the cable way that climbs to the top of table mountain, where tourists are able to enjoy the scenery and take in the beauty of Cape Town.

Robben Island, which is where Nelson Mandela was exiled for many years, is also one of the most famous tourist attractions. Tourists are now able to visit the prison and tour the cells where people were exiled and tortured for almost 400years. There are also speed boats and canoes made available for tourists to travel to the robben island which can also be a lot of fun for the whole family.
